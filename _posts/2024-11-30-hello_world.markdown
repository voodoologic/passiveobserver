## The Blog

I am making a blog to document the world in which I live.
Expect a bunch of posts about Emacs, k3s, self hosting, politics and various other topics.
This site is run on [jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/).  I thought about a mastodon micro-blog, but it's a lot more work than jekyll in this moment.
My aim is to host my own cloud using kubernetes.  It's taken 3 years to finally get my head around the intricacies
but this time around I've groked it enough that I want to take notes while I ship things.
This blog is that first step.

## The r320

I bought an r320 on [reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/homelabsales) a while back.
It's noisy, and I didn't know what to do with it.
Having recently upgraded the hard drives in my NAS, the r320 now had 4 cool "new" drives.
The OS is going to be [Security Onion](https://securityonionsolutions.com/).  It's on a 1TB volume with RAID 1.
The other volume is 5TB RAID 0 for packet ingestion.
I have no idea how to get this working, but at least the hardware is set up.
